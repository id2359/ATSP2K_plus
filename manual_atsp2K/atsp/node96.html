levels

Program to sort the energy levels in a name.l or name.j file and print energy levels in atomic units and cm, relative to the lowest. 

#  ........Processing energy level data........
>levels
 Enter name and type (.l or .j) of file
>A.j
  Default Rydberg constant (y/n)
>y
