Program description

The program performs the following data manipulation:
  • Energy differences. Compute the energy differences: computed-observed, and tabulate the data for the the entire sequence.
  • LS accuracies. Compute the LS trends and the LS accuracies for all transitions.
  • LSJ uncertainties. For each LSJ transition, the uncertainty is calculated from the combined error in transition energy and the error caused by LS discrepancy.
