Introduction

When the goal of atsp transition calculations is to calculate the properties of a given electronic sequence for a range of atoms, and for each atom to include a portion of the spectra, calculations may produce overwhelming amount of data. The analysis of data could become a daunting task. sa is designed to alleviate this task by performing most energy related analyses, including energy accuracies and transition uncertainties caused by errors of the computed vs. observed energies.
